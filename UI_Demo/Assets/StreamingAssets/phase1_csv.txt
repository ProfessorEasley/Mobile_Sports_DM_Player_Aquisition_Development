FORMULAS (Validated):
- Common streak: frustration += 5.0 × 1.2^(streak-1)
- Rare+ pull: satisfaction += 2.0 × 1.2^(streak-1) with +20% rare boost
              frustration -= 3.0 × 1.2^(streak-1) with NO rare boost
- Drought: frustration += 3.0 × 1.2^(drought_counter-1) every 10s
- Decay: frustration -1.5 (XP/dupe), satisfaction -2.0 (no rare+ 2 sessions)

GUARDRAILS:
- Soft cap: ~85% of target per update (max +8.5 for 0-10 range)
- Rare boost: ≤ +20% on satisfaction only
- Quiet window: 6-8s global after any hook fires
- Session reset: All emotions and streaks → 0
